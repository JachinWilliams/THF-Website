About THF

The Hotlist Forge is a team founded by Leo Stride but was handed over to John Doe due to Leo Stride not being active enough to do it in the subsequent years.

In 2023 Leo Stride, or LS, got together seven good but inactive or semi-active coders that would each make one program. After everyone was finished, they each released their program on one of the days of the week so that there would be one high-quality program on the hotlist each day for one week.

From 2024 onwards, John Doe is going to manage it and keep to LS' original idea. He created the original homepage to keep track of each years participants and projects. He also created the organization's logo (Top left). In 2025 the homepage was revamped by Jachin Williams.
